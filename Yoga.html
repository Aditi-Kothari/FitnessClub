When Yoga?

The best time to do yoga is early morning before breakfast.The second most perfect time is early evening,around sunset.

Yoga is amazing—even if you only practice for one hour a week, you will experience the benefits of the practice. If you can do more than that, you will certainly experience more benefits. I suggest starting with two or three times a week, for an hour or an hour and a half each time.

Things You Need For Yoga?

  • Your Own Mat.
  • Comfortable, Breathable Clothing.
  • A Good Water Bottle.
  • Don't Stress!

Types Of Yoga :To Choose Right One For You

Vinyasa Yoga

Who Need To: Anyone who wants more movement and less stillness from their yoga practice.

Hatha Yoga

Who Need To: Anyone looking for a balanced practice, or those in search of a gentler type of yoga.

Ashtanga Yoga

Who Need To:Anyone who likes routine or a more physical yet spiritual practice.

Yin Yoga

Who Need To: Those who need to stretch out after a tough workout, or anyone interested in a slower-paced practice.

Iyengar Yoga

Who Need To:Someone who likes detailed instruction, anyone with physical limitations, or those in search of a more classical form of yoga.

Power Yoga

Who Need To:Those who like ashtanga but want less rigidity, anyone who wants a good workout, and anyone who wants a less spiritual yoga practice.
